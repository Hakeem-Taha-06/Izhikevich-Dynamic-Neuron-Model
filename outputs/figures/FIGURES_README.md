# Figures Directory

## What Goes Here

This folder stores **all generated plots and visualizations** produced by the pipeline. Every team member who generates a figure should save it here with a descriptive filename.

### Expected Figures

| Filename | Generated By | Description |
|----------|-------------|-------------|
| `trajectory_comparison.png` | Role 9 (Evaluator) | Overlay plot of V(t) from all four solvers (LSODA, RK4, Backward Euler, PINN) |
| `gating_variables.png` | Role 9 (Evaluator) | Subplot comparison of m(t), h(t), n(t) across solvers |
| `bifurcation_diagram.png` | Role 10 (Crash Tester) | Bifurcation diagram: steady-state V vs. I_ext for all solvers |
| `training_loss_curve.png` | Role 8 (Training Operator) | PINN training loss over epochs |
| `rk4_stability_test.png` | Role 4 (RK4 Dev) | *(Optional)* Plot showing RK4 divergence at large dt |

### Naming Convention
- Use **lowercase with underscores**: `trajectory_comparison.png` ✅
- Avoid spaces or special characters: `Trajectory Comparison.png` ❌
- Use `.png` format for all plots (high resolution, `dpi=300` recommended).

### Git Policy
- Figures are **git-ignored** by default (see `.gitignore`).
- The `.gitkeep` ensures this folder exists in the repo.
- To share figures with the team, share via the team whatsapp group, zip and share.
